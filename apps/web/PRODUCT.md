# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Movie and series viewers who want a focused daily companion for discovering titles, tracking episodes and films, keeping a diary, rating what they watch, and organizing or sharing watchlists.

## Product Purpose

Kino gives personal viewing activity a calm, coherent home across web and mobile. It should make logging progress, finding the next thing to watch, and understanding a title or viewing history feel immediate and dependable.

## Brand Personality

Calm, focused, and companionable. The product is confident without being loud, cinematic without becoming ornamental, and concise without feeling cold.

## Anti-references

Avoid noisy streaming-service merchandising, gamified tracking dashboards, decorative motion, and visual departures from Kino's restrained dark surfaces and green action language. Do not trade familiar controls or readable information hierarchy for novelty.

## Design Principles

- Keep the user's viewing task in the foreground.
- Make progress and state changes immediately legible.
- Use hierarchy and spacing to clarify dense media metadata.
- Reserve delight for meaningful moments such as completing a season or series.
- Preserve a consistent interaction vocabulary across web and mobile.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain keyboard-visible focus, semantic labels, readable contrast, responsive layouts from 320px upward, localization-ready copy, and reduced-motion behavior for nonessential animation.
